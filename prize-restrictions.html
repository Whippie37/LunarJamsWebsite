Payout Information

In the event that your entry places within the top 5 scorers of a Jam, you will be contacted by myself (@whippie37 on discord) within 24 hours of the results being published to discuss how the prizes will be distributed. You will be contacted a second time after 7 days if no response is received within that time. Failure to respond with 31 days of the initial message will result in the prize being considered forfeit, and no payout will be given.

In the event that an entry created by multiple participants places within the top 5 entries, the participant who submitted the entry will be contacted first, and will be given the option to receive the full owed payout themselves to then distribute to their collab members as they see fit, or receive only their due share of the payout, and have each participant directly receive their portion of the prize. Prize restrictions apply to individual participants.

Restrictions

The following restrictions apply to prizes and payouts:

  1. Due to regional restrictions, participants based in Russia are unable to receive a direct monetary payout of any amount regardless of placement. In the event that a participant based in Russia scores within the top 5 entries, they will be provided a service or product of their choice equal in value to the deserved winnings. (i.e. steam game, gift card, product license, etc.)


  2. Due to overseas transfer fees, participants based outside of the United States must have some method of payment transfer app other than a direct bank transfer (i.e. PayPal, CashApp, Venmo, etc.). In the event that a winner based outside of the United States does not have a suitable method to recieve the prize, they will be given the option to receive their prize through a service or product of their choice equal in value to the deserved winnings (i.e. Steam game, gift card, product license, etc.). Should the participant decline the alternative prize payout, their winnings will be considered forfeit and no payout will be given.


  3. In the event that Lunar Jam organizers deem a participant unfit to receive a prize for any reason, we reserve the right to withold any prizes indefinitely.
